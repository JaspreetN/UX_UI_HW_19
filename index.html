Hi, I'm Jaspreet!

Transitioning from a premedical background, I discovered my passion for UX design as a way to channel my creativity and problem-solving skills into a more dynamic and user-focused field.

My journey from the sciences to design reflects my commitment to exploring diverse disciples and finding a path where empathy, innovation, and user-centric thinking converge.

Now, armed with a unique blend of analytical thinking and creative flair, I am dedicated to crafting seamless digital experiences that prioritize the human element in technology.

My UX Skills

User Research

My approach to user research includes a methodical and iterative process of understanding user behaviors, needs, preferences, and pain points through user and usability testing. 

User Empathy

Creating user personas, empathy maps, journey mapping, and user scenarios are essential in developing user-centric solutions and reduce designer biases in defining the user problem and ideating.

Information Architecture

My methods include conducting heuristic evaluations, content auditing, card-sorting, and site-mapping. I follow these steps with usability testing to discover where to focus iterations in the design process.

My Work

Wander Whiz

WanderWhiz targets the problem of busy travelers needing an easy and flexible way to plan their days efficiently to do more activities within their limited time. As an app that uses AI to generate an itinerary for users’ specific travel destinations based on their preferences.

Closet Cue

Closet Cue is designed for individuals needing a plan when selecting an outfit within their existing closet for different occasions. Its design was optimized to fit their style goals to eliminate the stress and time it takes to curate an outfit. It also has an AI feature utilizes that generates outfits based on preferences.

The Pepper Foundation

The redesign of the Pepper Foundation focused on information architecture due to the lack of organization and consistency. Another focus was introducing more positive imagery and responsive web design with a goal of encouraging confidence in users and potential pet adoptees.
